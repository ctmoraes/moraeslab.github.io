SUPPORT

Biomedical research is technology-driven and requires substantial funds to achieve its goals. In our lab the goal is to develop treatments and cures for mitochondrial diseases. Towards achieving these goals we have been helped by grants from the NIH and foundations and by generous families afflicted with mitochondrial disorders. If you would like to help with our effort and speed up our research, please contact Dr. Moraes. Below we list our past and present supporters.

Family Support

The JM Fund (Mileti Family); The Biscardi Family; The Esther Lichtenstein Chair.

Grant Support – Foundations

The Muscular Dystrophy Association; The United Mitochondrial Disease Foundation; American Heart Association; The Florida Biomedical Foundation; The CHAMP Foundation.

Grant Support – Federal

National Eye Institute (NEI); National Institute on Aging (NIA); National Institute of Neurological Disorders and Stroke (NINDS); National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences (NIEHS); Army Research Office.

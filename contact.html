Prospective Students and Postdocs

We are proud to have a friendly and collaborative environment in the lab.

Graduate Students: Interested Ph.D. students should enter the Program in Biological Sciences (PIBS), an umbrella program that allows students to choose between different programs once they select a lab. Dr. Moraes is part of the following programs: Neuroscience, Molecular Cell and Developmental Biology, and Human Genetics and Genomics.

Postdoctoral Fellows: Interested postdoctoral fellows should contact Dr. Moraes directly by sending a letter and CV by email.
